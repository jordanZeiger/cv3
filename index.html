How to Protect Yourself & Others

Wash your hands often

Wash your hands with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.

Avoid close contact

Stay at least SIX feet from other people.

Cover your mouth and nose

You can spread the virus even if you don't feel sick. Everyone should wear face coverings when in public, but wearing a mask does NOT mean you can get close to others.

Cover coughs and sneezes

Throw used tissues in the trash, and then IMMEDIATELY wash your hands.

Clean and disinfect

Clean and disinfect surfaces DAILY.

Monitor Your Health

Be alert for symptoms and take your temperature if they develop.
